The Next Ten Years

Over the next 10 years Target has some major challenges ahead of itself

The Mainframe

For the last 25 years our applications have been built on the IBM Mainframe platform. This not only has a huge cost, since we pay IBM for space and computational power, it also has a cost in finding resources to support this aging platform and enticing young talent to come to target. For these reasons there is a big push to migrate off the mainframe and onto new technologies.

Application conversion

We're currently building out brand new applications across the entire company in the newest technologies. This effort is looking at each area, determining which applications are absolutely mandatory, and converting those applications to new platforms.

.com

Growing our .com business will be a big priority over the next 10 years. Every year for the last 5 years target.com has gained marketshare in that space. We will continue that trend and at the same time look for ways to leverage our stores so online sales are not done at a loss. Every major retailer today operates their online business at a loss, Target is looking to reverse that trend in unique ways that only a major brick and mortar retailer can.
