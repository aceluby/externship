Engineering at Target

Even though we're mainly a brick and mortar retailer, the entire company is run via technology, it's not just online sales.

Aplications

  • Forecast applications to determine what we're going to need before we need it that takes sales, promotions, and human input into account.
  • Order applications so we can send orders to vendors and track those orders until receipt
  • Transportation booking and customs management systems
  • Allocation algorithms to ensure the product gets to the store in order to sell it
  • Applications to help break down trucks and get the product to the right shelf
  • Point of sale applications for self-checkout and Team Member checkout
  • Inventory applications so we know what product is where at any given moment
  • Planning applications for promotions, peak products (like eggs during easter), and seasonal planning
  • Vendor and item applications that store all the info we would need about a specific vendor and product
  • Many, many more....

Agile Scrum

Target is an agile workplace, specifically agile scrum. Below is a list of what constitutes an agile scrum team:

  • Teams are small 3-8 people per team.
  • Work is broken into Epics and Stories
    • Epics are large bodies of work
    • Stories are tiny bodies of work
    • Stories are tied to an epic
  • As stories are identified they are put onto the backlog
  • The backlog is prioritized by business need
  • Stories at the top of the backlog are pointed according to their complexity
    • Each team will point differently, points are only to manage work on the team
  • Work is planned in 2 week increments called sprints
  • Sprints are planned based on what has been completed in previous sprints
    • Average 50 points a sprint, a sprint plan should be ~50 points planned
  • Ceremonies happen to groom/point the backlog, review the previous sprint, and plan the next sprint
  • New work is always put on the backlog first to go through the grooming/planning process
  • Defects are added directly to the sprint
